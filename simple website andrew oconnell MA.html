One of my all time favourite movies

The Shawshank Redemption


Year -> 1994


Notable Awards -> 5 Oscar nominations including best picture


Staring -> Morgan Freeman & Tim Robbins


The reason I chose this film was because it was an iconic film of its era. I think even today in 2024 it does not seem dated and very much has stood the test of time.


It is a story of hope and triumph of the human spirit over unimaginable adversity. It was written by a very unlikely source in the powerhouse of horror fiction that is Stephen King.


It has a rich plot with some unexpected twists and turns. I believe it warrants a position up their with the greatest films of all time
